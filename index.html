Biography

I am now a third-year PhD student from DataHammerGroup in Beijing Institute of Technology, supervised by Assoc. Prof. Xian-Ling Mao. I received my bachelor degree in Department of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Institute of Technology in 2018. My major research interests are in information retrieval and vision-language pretraining.
